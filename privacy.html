Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Visionary is a personal productivity tool built by a student, currently in early development. This policy explains how your data is handled.

What we collect

Visionary collects nothing on any server. All of your tasks, reflections, streaks, and history are stored locally on your own device using your browser's localStorage.

What that means

Your data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded, shared, sold, or viewed by anyone — including the creators of Visionary. If you clear your browser data, your Visionary history is deleted.

Third-party services

Visionary uses Google Fonts to load typography and a CDN to load charting code. These providers may log basic request information (like your IP address) when your browser fetches these resources. This is standard for almost every website.

Future changes

If Visionary adds features that require sending data to a server (such as user accounts or cross-device sync), this policy will be updated and you will be notified clearly before that happens.

Your rights

Because all data lives on your device, you have full control. You can clear your Visionary data at any time through your browser's settings, or by clearing site data for this domain.

Contact

Visionary is built by a college student as a solo project. If you have questions, you can reach out at louisl4764@gmail.com.
