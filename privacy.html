Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Visionary is a personal productivity tool built by a college student, currently in early development. This policy explains how your data is handled.

What we collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address (or Google profile info if you sign in with Google). When you use the app, we store the tasks you add and the reflections you log.

Where it's stored

Your account and your data are stored on Supabase, a hosting platform that powers the backend. Communication between your browser and the server is encrypted using HTTPS.

Passwords

Your password is never stored in readable form. It's encrypted using a one-way hash before it ever touches our database. Even the developer cannot view it. If you sign in with Google, no password is created at all.

Who can see your data

Other users cannot see your data. Database security rules ensure each account only has access to its own tasks and reflections. The developer (the person building this app) can technically access the database, which means task and reflection content is visible to them. Please don't enter sensitive personal information you wouldn't want them to read.

Tracking and analytics

Visionary does not use third-party tracking, advertising, or behavioral analytics tools. We do not sell, trade, or share your data with anyone.

Your rights

You can stop using Visionary at any time. If you want your account and data deleted, contact the developer (info below) and it will be removed within a few days.

Future changes

If Visionary adds features that meaningfully change how data is handled (such as sharing features, AI training on user data, or third-party integrations), this policy will be updated and you'll be notified before any change takes effect.

Contact

Visionary is built by a college student as a solo project. For questions or to request data deletion, reach out at louisl4764@gmail.com.
